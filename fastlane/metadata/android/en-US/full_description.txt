Alauncher is a modern, minimalist Android launcher designed for users who want a clean and futuristic home screen experience.

Features:
• Dynamic neon and minimalist themes with full customization
• Orbital and standard app drawer styles
• Full support for native Android widgets
• Fluid gestures for search, notifications and app drawer
• Icon pack support with monochromatic icon tinting
• Music player widget with MediaSession integration
• Weather display on the home screen
• Customizable clock styles (digital, analog, strip)
• Drag and drop to organize apps and create folders
• Double tap to sleep
• Global search with app and contact results
• Tablet and landscape mode support
• Material Design with smooth animations
• Lightweight and fast

Alauncher respects your privacy. No ads, no tracking, no data collection. Fully open source under GPL-3.0 license.
